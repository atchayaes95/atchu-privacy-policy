Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 25, 2026

Atchu's Aura ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal identification information. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Physical addresses
  • Precise location data

Local Storage (Room Database)

The app uses an internal Room database to store daily predictions and user preferences locally on your device. This data:

  • Is stored exclusively on your device.
  • Is used to provide offline access to predictions.
  • Is not uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

Firebase Firestore

We use Firebase Firestore (a service provided by Google) to synchronize daily predictions and application data.

  • Firestore stores the content for predictions and weekday images.
  • Usage of Firestore is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

2. App Features and Behavior

Offline Usage

Atchu's Aura is designed to function without a continuous internet connection. Once data is synced from Firestore, the app stores it locally for offline viewing.

Notifications

The app sends daily notifications at 6:00 AM to provide your daily prediction. You can enable or disable these notifications at any time through your device settings.

Permissions

We request only the minimum necessary permissions to provide app functionality:

🌐
Internet Access
Required to sync predictions from Firestore.
🔔
Post Notifications
Required to send daily prediction alerts (Android 13+).
⏰
Schedule Exact Alarms
Required to ensure notifications trigger precisely at 6:00 AM.
We do not request access to sensitive permissions like Camera, Microphone, Contacts, or Location.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the data stored locally on your device. However, as the data is stored on your device, its security also depends on your own device security measures.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.
